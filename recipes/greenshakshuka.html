Vegan Green Shakshuka

Try this delicious twist on a classic shakshuka, using green veg for a healthy, earthy flavour and tofu egg to make this dish completely plant-based.

Ingredients

  • 1 leek, chopped
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 1 large courgette, sliced
  • 200g spinach
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 200g tofu
  • 1/2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp nooch
  • A pinch of black salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Sautee leeks, onion and courgette in a pan until soft
  3. Add spices, garlic and spinach, cook until wilted
  4. Squeeze in lemon and stir
  5. Blend tofu and other ingredients to create the 'egg'
  6. Blend half of the green mixture and add back to pan
  7. Top the mixture with blobs of the tofu mix
  8. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes, until the tofu has firmed up
  9. Top with avocado, fresh parsley, sliced spring onion and chilli oil
  10. Serve with toasted sourdough
